# Excel Sales Analysis

## Problem Statement
To analyze sales and profit data to identify trends, profitability drivers, and the impact of discounting over time.

## Dataset
Sample Superstore Sales Dataset

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts

## Analysis Performed
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Product Category
- Discount Impact on Profit
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

## Key Insights
- West region is the highest sales contributor.
- Technology category generates the highest profit.
- Higher discounts significantly reduce profit margins.
- Sales show seasonal trends with periodic peaks and mid-year dips.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus expansion efforts on high-performing regions.
- Promote high-margin Technology products.
- Limit aggressive discounting to protect profitability.
- Improve demand forecasting based on seasonal sales patterns.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates the use of Excel analytics to derive actionable business insights for data-driven decision-making.
